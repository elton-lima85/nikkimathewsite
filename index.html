About Dr. Nikki

A specialist who truly listens and cares

Dr. Nikki Mathew is a qualified neurophysiotherapist with 4+ years of clinical experience across leading hospitals in Pune. Co-founder of Soma Rehab, she specialises in adult and paediatric neurological rehabilitation, helping patients of all ages regain movement, function, and confidence.

  • BPT – Dr. D. Y. Patil College of Physiotherapy, Pune
  • MPT (Neurophysiotherapy) – Sancheti Institute of Orthopaedics & Rehabilitation, Pune
  • Clinical experience at Capri Spine Clinic, Saishree Hospital & Deenanath Mangeshkar Hospital
  • Adult & Paediatric Neuro Rehab — treating patients across all age groups
  • Home visits available in Aundh, Baner & nearby areas (prior appointment mandatory)

Conditions Treated

Neurological conditions we specialise in

Evidence-based rehabilitation for a wide range of neurological and neuromuscular conditions.

🩺

Stroke Rehabilitation

Comprehensive rehab targeting motor recovery, balance, coordination, and activities of daily living after ischemic or hemorrhagic stroke.

🔄

Parkinson's Disease

Specialized movement therapy to improve gait, reduce freezing episodes, enhance posture, and maintain functional independence.

⚡

Multiple Sclerosis

Fatigue management, balance training, spasticity reduction, and functional mobility strategies tailored for MS progression.

🧩

Traumatic Brain Injury

Post-TBI rehabilitation addressing motor control, cognitive-motor integration, dizziness, and return-to-function goals.

🦴

Spinal Cord Conditions

Rehabilitation for myelopathy, incomplete SCI, and post-surgical spinal recovery focusing on regaining mobility and strength.

🤝

Peripheral Neuropathy

Targeted therapy for nerve damage causing weakness, sensory loss, and balance deficits to restore functional independence.
